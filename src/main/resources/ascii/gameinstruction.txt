                         ===========Game Objective===========
It’s the year 2050, and a virus called “Jackpot” is killing the population to extinction.
You have 8 chances to travel by portal to 4 locations, you must trade for:

    1. Camu Camu
    2. Camel Milk
    3. Sumalak
    4. Glacier Magical Plant

Bring them back to the laboratory to Dr. WW to create an antidote.

Start your mission at Area 51, with a bag that can only holds 4 items. Good Luck!
